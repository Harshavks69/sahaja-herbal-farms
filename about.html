Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Sahaja Herbal Farms

What makes Sahaja eggs different from regular eggs?

+

Our hens are fed with 100% organic, vegetarian feed and have access to free-range environments. They drink ozonized water and live in pollution-free surroundings, resulting in healthier, more nutritious eggs with better taste and longer shelf life.

Are your farming practices truly organic?

+

Yes, we are certified organic by national standards. We use no antibiotics, chemicals, or harmful additives. Our hens eat only vegetarian, organic feed and live in natural, stress-free environments.

How do you ensure the freshness of your products?

+

We collect eggs daily and maintain a cold chain throughout our supply process. Our products are delivered within 24-48 hours of collection, ensuring maximum freshness and quality.

Do you offer home delivery services?

+

Yes, we provide home delivery services across Andhra Pradesh. We also offer subscription plans for regular customers who want fresh eggs delivered weekly or bi-weekly.

Can I visit your farm to see the facilities?

+

Absolutely! We encourage farm visits and offer guided tours on weekends. Please contact us in advance to schedule your visit and learn about our sustainable farming practices firsthand.

What are your quality certifications?

+

We hold ISO 22000 certification for food safety management, organic certification from national standards, and various awards for excellence in animal welfare and sustainable farming practices.
