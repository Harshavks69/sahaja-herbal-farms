Our Hen & Farm Story

A journey of passion, care, and natural farming

Where Nature Meets Nurture

At Sahaja Herbal Farms, our story began with a simple belief - that the healthiest eggs come from the happiest hens. Our journey started when we realized that conventional farming methods weren't aligned with our values of natural, sustainable agriculture.

We created a haven where our hens can express their natural behaviors, roam freely in lush herbal gardens, and enjoy a diet rich in medicinal herbs and organic grains. Each hen is treated as an individual, with love and respect for their natural instincts.

Our farm is more than just a business - it's a commitment to providing families with the purest, most nutritious eggs while maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare and environmental sustainability.
